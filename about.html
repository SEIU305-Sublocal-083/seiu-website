About Local 083

SEIU Local 503 at Oregon State University

Local 083 is our union for classified staff at Oregon State University.

We represent coworkers who support students, research and public service in every Oregon county. As part of SEIU Local 503, we bargain fair contracts, defend workplace rights and set our priorities together at OSU.

Is this my union?

Local 083 represents classified staff across OSU.

If your position is in the classified bargaining unit, Local 083 negotiates and enforces the contract that covers your work. We work in offices, kitchens, labs, shops, residence halls, classrooms, farms, forests and extension offices throughout Oregon.

Who does Local 083 represent at OSU?

Local 083 represents classified staff across Oregon State University, including administrative staff, food service workers, custodians, IT professionals, researchers, tradespeople and many other coworkers who keep OSU running.

What is the difference between being represented and being a member?

Workers in our bargaining unit are covered by our contract. Full members also have a direct vote in contract ratification and union elections and can take a fuller role in setting priorities and building our union.

What does a union steward do?

Stewards are trained coworkers who help members understand the contract, prepare for workplace meetings and address concerns with management.

How are decisions made in Local 083?

Members shape priorities through workplace conversations, surveys, meetings and votes. Members elect local leaders and vote on tentative agreements.
