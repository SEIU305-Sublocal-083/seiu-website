BARGAINING KICKOFF

2026 Bargaining Kickoff: Why This Year Matters

Published on January 9, 2026

Corvallis, OR — This isn't just about a contract; it's about our livelihood and the future of our work at OSU. As we enter 2026, we face a critical opportunity to define what "fairness" means for classified staff at Oregon State University.

New year, new contract fight. The results from our recent "Fighting for Higher Ed" survey were loud and clear. With record-high participation, members across every department signaled that the status quo is no longer acceptable. You told us that stagnant wages in the face of rising living costs are unsustainable.

Our Priorities

Based on your feedback, our bargaining team has set a clear agenda for the upcoming negotiations:

  • Real COLA: We are fighting for a Cost of Living Adjustment that actually keeps up with inflation.
  • Benefit Protections: Defending our healthcare and retirement from any proposed cuts.
  • Workload Equity: Addressing the increasing demands placed on staff without adequate support.
"We are proud public employees, not just 'staff.' This year, we fight to ensure the university recognizes the value we bring every single day." — SEIU 503 Bargaining Team
