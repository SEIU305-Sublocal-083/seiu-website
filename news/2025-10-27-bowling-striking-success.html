COMMUNITY EVENT

Bowling Night Was a Striking Success!

Published on Monday October 27, 2025

Kary Lines Up a Shot!

Our sublocal Secretary, Kary, shows perfect form on the lane.

Friendly Chats

Our President, Russ, and Membership Coordinator, Gino, discuss how to strengthen our union.

Building Connections

Chief Steward Patrick (in purple) and Vice President Richard chat about union matters.

We had an absolute blast at our union bowling night! It was fantastic to see so many members come out for an evening of friendly competition, laughter, and solidarity. We traded our work shoes for bowling shoes, enjoyed some pizza, and cheered each other on, whether we were throwing strikes or gutter balls.

Events like these are what our union is all about—building strong connections with our colleagues outside of the workplace. When we know each other, we build the trust and relationships that make us a more powerful and united force when it comes to fighting for our rights and our contract.

More Than Just a Game

Our strength comes from our unity, and that unity is built one conversation, one shared laugh, and one bowling game at a time. Thank you to everyone who organized and attended. We can't wait to see you at the next event!
