BARGAINING UPDATE

Make Your Voice Heard! The 2026 Bargaining Survey is Here!

Last updated: October 1, 2025

Created: 2025-10-01

Published: 2025-10-01

It's time to set our priorities for our next contract! The official SEIU 503 bargaining survey for our 2026 contract negotiations is now open to all members. This is your chance to tell your elected bargaining team what matters most to you in our workplace.

Your confidential responses will directly guide our proposals at the negotiating table with OSU management. Whether your top priority is wages, healthcare, workplace safety, or a better work-life balance, we need to hear from you. The more members who participate, the stronger our message will be.

"This survey is our most powerful tool for building a bargaining platform that reflects the needs of every member. Your voice matters, and we need to hear it." — Jax Johnson, Communications Chair

What Happens Next?

Once the survey closes, our bargaining team will analyze the results to identify the top priorities for our membership. These priorities will form the foundation of our proposals to management as we begin the bargaining process for our next contract.

Please take a few minutes to complete the survey today. Encourage your coworkers who are members to do the same. If you know someone who isn't a member, now is the perfect time for them to join so they can have a say in our contract!
