BARGAINING UPDATE

Your Voice, Our Contract: Bargaining Survey Update

Last updated: November 3, 2025

Created: 2025-11-03

Published: 2025-11-03

As we gear up for the next bargaining season, the strength of our union comes from each and every one of us. Hearing from you is the most critical step in preparing our proposals for a fair contract with. Our bargaining team is already hard at work, and your input directly shapes the priorities we bring to the table.

“The Bargaining Team met and reviewed the current survey results! The survey is still open so continue to encourage your fellow union member to fill out the survey. As we begin to work on the contract and prepare for the upcoming bargaining season; it’s imperative that we continue to encourage each other in ways to be involved in our union, so that we can show up and show out as a strong unified force as early as possible. Our union is us, so let’s be union strong.” — Donatellia Austin, Bargaining Committee Member

Have you completed the survey yet? It only takes a few minutes and is the best way to make your voice heard. You can find the link to the survey in your email, or contact a steward if you need help finding it. Let's make sure our bargaining team has the feedback they need to win the contract we all deserve!

Can't find your Survey?

Please check your inbox for an email with the title "It's time for your 2025 Higher Ed Bargaining Survey" from alert@seiu503.org. Your confidential feedback will directly shape our team's priorities.

Can't find the email? Let us know so we can help!
