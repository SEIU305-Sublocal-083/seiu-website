CONTRACT UPDATE

Our COLA Increase Is Here!

Published on August 22, 2025 | Last updated on August 22, 2025

A friendly reminder to all of us: the cost-of-living adjustment (COLA) that we won together in our recently ratified contract takes effect this month. You should see this important increase reflected in your upcoming paycheck.

This victory is a direct result of our collective action and solidarity. Every one of us who wore purple, attended a meeting, or talked to a coworker helped send a clear message to university management: we deserve wages that keep up with the rising cost of living.

What This Means For Us

Our contract secures a guaranteed **3.5% COLA** for all classified staff in the first year, with another increase scheduled for next year. This ensures that our hard-earned pay won't lose value to inflation, providing much-needed financial stability for ourselves and our families.

Questions About Your Pay?

We encourage everyone to check their pay stubs to ensure the increase has been correctly applied. If you have any questions or feel there is an issue, please don't hesitate to reach out to a department steward or contact our union office for support. We're here to help.

We're Stronger Together

If you have any questions about our new contract or your pay, please reach out. We have your back.
