CONTRACT VICTORY & CALL TO ACTION

Union Power Delivers: Your Final 3% COLA Raise Arrives November 1st!

Published on October 18, 2025

Get ready for another increase in your paycheck! The final cost-of-living adjustment (COLA) that we won together in our current contract takes effect on November 1, 2025. All classified staff will see a 3% raise reflected in their November paycheck.

This is a big win and a direct result of our collective action and solidarity. Let's take a moment to look at what we've accomplished together with this contract, which has resulted in a cumulative compounded raise of nearly 16%!

A Look Back at Our Historic Wins

The upcoming 3% raise is the capstone on a series of significant gains. Since our contract was ratified, our unity has secured:

  • A $1,500 one-time payment (April 2024)
  • A 6.5% COLA (April 1, 2024)
  • A 2% COLA (November 1, 2024)
  • A 3.5% COLA (June 1, 2025)

Who Gets This Raise?

To be clear: every classified employee will receive this raise. If you are employed by the university in a classified position on November 1, 2025, you are entitled to the 3% COLA, even if you were hired just a few days prior. It's a benefit for all of us.

Share Your Story, Build Our Power

This raise makes a real difference. What will it mean for you and your family? Paying off a bill, saving for the future, or just having a little less stress at the grocery store? Sharing our stories shows the real-world impact of our collective power. Talk to your steward or a coworker about what this raise means to you. Our numbers and our stories give us strength for the next fight.

Shape Our Next Contract: Take the Bargaining Survey!

As we celebrate this win, we must prepare for the next round of negotiations. It is crucial that we hear from you!

Please check your inbox for an email with the title "It's time for your 2025 Higher Ed Bargaining Survey" from alert@seiu503.org. Your confidential feedback will directly shape our priorities.

Not a member yet? Your voice is still essential. Join Us Today and take the survey to make sure your priorities are heard!

Questions About Your Pay?

We encourage everyone to check their pay stubs in November to ensure the 3% increase has been correctly applied. If you have any questions or believe there is an error, please don't hesitate to reach out to a department steward or contact our union office for support. We're here to help.

We're Stronger Together

Take the bargaining survey, check your paycheck in November, and let's keep building our power.
