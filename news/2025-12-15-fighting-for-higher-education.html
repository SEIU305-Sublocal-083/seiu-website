BARGAINING UPDATE

Fighting for Higher Education

Published on December 15, 2025

Add your Name TODAY!

We as Classified workers at Oregon’s Public Universities, we are the foundation of higher education! We unite in support of bargaining priorities you submitted to our bargaining team.

Now take the next step and sign the Higher Education Petition to tell management we MUST have these priorities in our next contract:

  • Economics: Fair wages that keep up with cost of living.
  • Benefit Protections: Affordable health insurance and strong PERS contributions.
  • Workload: Sustainable workloads, remote work equity, and employment protections.
  • Union Power: Mandatory New employee orientations, union communication access to new hires, and stronger layoff language.
