NOTICE TO FELLOW UNION MEMBERS

July 16 membership meeting moved to MU 215

By Jax SN Johnson, President, SEIU Local 503 Sublocal 083

Published: July 10, 2026

Created: 2026-07-10

Last updated: 2026-07-10

Fellow union members,

Next week's General Membership Meeting will be held in Memorial Union room 215, not our usual room, MU 211.

The Memorial Union double-booked our usual room and found an alternate space for us in MU 215. The meeting is still Thursday, July 16, from noon to 1 p.m.

We have also ordered TOGO'S sandwiches for lunch.

Please head directly to MU 215 and share this room change with coworkers who plan to attend. We look forward to seeing you there.

In solidarity,
Jax SN Johnson
President, SEIU Local 503 Sublocal 083
