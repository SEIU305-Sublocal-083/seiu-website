LEADERSHIP UPDATE

Introducing Our New Sublocal 083 Leadership Team

By Jax SN Johnson, President & Comms Chair, SEIU 503 Sublocal 083

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Created: 2026-04-21

Published: 2026-04-21

SEIU 503 Sublocal 083 has shared its updated executive leadership team and authorized steward roster with Oregon State University's Employee and Labor Relations office. The note below is a lightly cleaned copy of the email sent to Anthony Rosilez, Director of Employee and Labor Relations, University Human Resources Central.

Memorandum

SEIU Sublocal 083, Oregon State University

To:

Anthony Rosilez, Director of Employee and Labor Relations

From:

Jax SN Johnson, President of SEIU 503, Sublocal 083

Date:

April 21, 2026

Subject:

Introducing Our New Sublocal 083 Leadership Team

Dear Anthony,

I am reaching out on behalf of our newly elected leadership team to formally introduce myself as the new President of SEIU Local 503, Sublocal 083, and to share our updated steward roster. As we both navigate these roles at Oregon State University, our team is eager to build a productive and communicative relationship with your office.

As someone who cares deeply about Oregon State University, I share the immense pride our members take in making this institution run. Our daily work is the foundation that helps ensure every student graduates. We believe that a strong, supported classified workforce is the cornerstone of a campus community where prosperity is truly widely shared, and we look forward to working collaboratively with you to make these shared goals a reality.

Executive Leadership

  • Jax Johnson, President
  • James Pavis, Vice President
  • Richard Keuneke, Chief Steward
  • J. (Jacob) Harold, Membership Coordinator
  • Gabriel Labarca, Secretary
  • Vinay Ramakrishnan, Treasurer

Authorized Stewards

Our current stewards are Anne Gross, Susan Clark, Jacqueline Reilly, Krystal Waldschmidt, Tracey Jalstad, Patrick Breshears, and Andrew Struthers.

Please let us know if you have any questions.

In solidarity,
Jax SN Johnson
