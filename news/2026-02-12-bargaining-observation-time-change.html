Bargaining Observation Time Update: 10:00 AM Moved to 1:15 PM

By SEIU 503 Sublocal 083, Union Leadership

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Created: 2026-02-12

Published: 2026-02-12

Important scheduling update: Today's bargaining Zoom observation was originally scheduled for 10:00 AM.

Around 9:00 AM, management changed the session time to 1:15 PM.

To make sure members have accurate information, we are sending email updates and have updated the website across our event and resource pages.

Please note the observation room rules remain in effect: members should stay muted, and anyone joining after 1:20 PM will be removed from the session.
