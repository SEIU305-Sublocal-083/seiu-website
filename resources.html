Deadlines can be short. Save written notices and relevant records, and involve a steward early when a situation is urgent or unclear.

If a meeting could lead to discipline

You can request union representation.

Ask whether the meeting could lead to discipline. If it could, clearly request a union representative and wait for representation before answering questions.

“I request union representation. I choose not to answer questions until my union representative is present.”
