Your Rights at Work

Weingarten Rights

You have the right to union representation during any investigatory interview that could lead to discipline. If your supervisor calls you into a meeting, ask if it could lead to discipline. If the answer is yes, you have the right to say:

"I request union representation. I refuse to answer questions until my union representative is present."

Grievance Procedure

If you believe management has violated the contract, you have the right to file a grievance. The first step is always to talk to your union steward. They are trained to help you navigate the process.
