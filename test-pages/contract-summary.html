Plain-Language Overview

Contract Summary (2022–2026)

This page is a plain-language guide to the SEIU Local 503 OSU contract. It highlights the major topics covered in the agreement and points you to the official PDF for the exact language.

Pay & Wages

The contract explains how pay is set for classified staff, including wage schedules, step progressions, and cost-of-living adjustments. For exact pay rates and timing, use the official PDF.

Overtime & Scheduling

Look here for how overtime is defined, how it is scheduled, and how it is paid. This section also covers schedule changes, notice requirements, and rules for shifts.

Leave & Time Off

The contract covers vacation, sick leave, holidays, and other leave types. It also includes guidance on requesting time off and how leave accrues.

Benefits

This section summarizes eligibility and participation for health coverage, retirement programs, and other benefit offerings. For enrollment deadlines and plan specifics, check the PDF or contact the union.

Safety & Working Conditions

Find the contract language on safe workplaces, protective equipment, and what to do when conditions are unsafe. It also covers how safety concerns are raised and addressed.

Discipline & Grievances

The contract defines just cause, discipline procedures, and the steps for filing grievances. It also outlines timelines and the role of union representation throughout the process.

Union Rights & Representation

This section covers member rights to union representation, access to stewards, and protections for union activity. It connects closely with Weingarten Rights and grievance procedures.

Classifications & Movement

The contract includes guidance on job classifications, reclassifications, and how movement between positions is handled. Use the PDF for the official definitions.
