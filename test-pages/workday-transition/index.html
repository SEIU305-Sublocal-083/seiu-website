Workday Transition Hub

This evergreen staging section helps us organize in real time during Oregon State University's transition from legacy systems to Workday. If something breaks, delays, or looks wrong, report it to our union even if you already reported it to HR or OSU support.

Training, Feedback, and Reporting

Where to find trainings and how to report concerns to OSU and our union.

Report everything. Build collective power.

One report helps one person. Many reports help our union identify patterns, protect members, and pressure management to fix system-level failures. If you solved your issue, still report it so others can learn from it.

How to report problems

Report to our union

  1. Save screenshots, dates, amounts, ticket numbers, and approval chain details.
  2. Email our union team with subject line: "Workday transition issue".
  3. Include urgency: missed pay, blocked work, grant risk, or routine correction.
  4. Report even if HR or OSU support is already working your ticket.
  5. Report even if your issue was fixed; your timeline can help another member.

Contact: 083stewards@seiu503.org

Submit feedback to OSU

  1. Use your department or central OSU Workday support pathway.
  2. Submit complete details once, then track ticket updates in writing.
  3. Share every update and final outcome with our union so trends can be tracked.
