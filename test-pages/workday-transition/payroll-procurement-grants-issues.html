Payroll, Procurement, and Grants Issues

Use this triage model to classify urgency, capture complete evidence, and route problems to OSU and our union. Report every issue, including issues already being handled, so our union can map patterns and fight for system fixes.

Issue triage by urgency

High

Missed pay, blocked payroll run, frozen purchasing needed for operations, or grant compliance deadline risk.

Medium

Incorrect coding, delayed reimbursements, approval routing errors, invoice queue failures.

Routine

Display errors, non-blocking data mismatches, and non-time-sensitive cleanup requests.

Evidence packet checklist

Open/close evidence checklist (check every box before sending)

Use one email or ticket thread. Each checkbox is required for a complete report.

Routing pattern

  1. Open OSU support ticket with full evidence packet.
  2. Escalate internally per department process when urgent.
  3. Notify our union with summary and ticket details for pattern tracking.
  4. Close the loop by sending final resolution and correction timeline.
  5. Encourage coworkers with similar issues to report to our union too.
