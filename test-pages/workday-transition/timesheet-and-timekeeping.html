Timesheet and Timekeeping

Use this checklist daily and weekly during the transition. Keep your own records so corrections can be made quickly, and report issues to our union even when HR or OSU support is already helping.

Daily capture checklist

  • Record start, stop, and meal break times outside Workday (note app, email draft, or personal log).
  • Capture screenshots after entering time, including date and hour totals.
  • If entries fail to save, note exact error text and timestamp.
  • Keep copies of schedule changes approved by supervisors.

Weekly review checklist

  • Compare logged hours to submitted hours line by line before approval cutoff.
  • Save a final screenshot of weekly totals and approval state.
  • Confirm overtime, differential, and shift premiums appear correctly.
  • Flag missing approvals early to avoid delayed pay.

If there is a mismatch

  1. Document issue type: missing hours, wrong rate, wrong code, or failed approval.
  2. Submit correction through OSU support channel and save ticket number.
  3. Email our union with your evidence packet and ticket number.
  4. Send follow-up to our union when resolved so other members can use your path.

Do not self-screen: if it affected you, report it to our union. If it almost affected you, report it to our union.
