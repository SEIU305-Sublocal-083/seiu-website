Welcome to Oregon State!

We are SEIU 503, our union of classified staff at OSU, and we're excited to have you here. This page is your starting point for learning about our union.

A Message from Our Union

On behalf of over a thousand of your new colleagues, welcome to Oregon State University! We are the dedicated employees who make this university work every single day. We are administrative assistants, IT professionals, custodians, lab technicians, and so much more.

As our union for classified staff, our mission is to ensure every one of us has the respect, fair pay, and safe working conditions we deserve. We do this by standing together to negotiate a legally binding contract with the university. We are a democratic, member-run organization, and our strength comes from our unity.

We invite you to join us and make our union even stronger.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a Member and a Non-member?

All classified employees are covered by the benefits and protections of the contract our union negotiates. However, only members can vote on the contract, elect our leadership, and run for office. Our power to win strong contracts comes from high membership.

How are dues used?

Union dues are an investment in our collective power. They fund everything we need to win strong contracts and defend our rights, including skilled negotiators, legal support, organizing resources, and training for volunteer stewards.

I have more questions, who can I talk to?

We're happy to answer any questions you have! The best people to talk to are your colleagues. You can also reach out to any of our elected union leaders or send a general inquiry through our Contact Page.
