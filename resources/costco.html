Costco Membership Bonus

Exclusive Offer for SEIU Members

Overview

Find what you need for home and work. Join Costco today! Receive a $40 Digital Costco Shop Card when you join as a new Executive Member, or a $20 Digital Costco Shop Card when you join as a new Gold Star Member and enroll in auto renewal of your annual membership.

For our members in Corvallis and surrounding areas, there is a Costco location nearby in Albany, OR.

Benefit Details

Costco offers a great value on what you need, all in one place.

  • Shop smart: A Costco membership grants you access to over 500 member-only warehouses nationwide, plus Costco.com.
  • Check off your list in one stop: Get your prescriptions filled at Costco Pharmacy or find exceptional value at Costco Optical and Costco Hearing Aid Center.
  • Upgrade your space: Costco Services helps make it easier to modernize your home from top to bottom. Save on home improvement products.
  • Get ready for the road: Replace your tires at Costco Tire Center and fill up your tank at Costco Gas Stations.
  • Enjoy friendly member service: Costco Technical and Warranty Services offers free tech support on select electronics.

How to Get This

  1. Make sure you’re signed in to your SEIU Member Benefits account.
  2. Click the “Join Now” button on the SEIU Member Benefits page to purchase your Costco membership.
  3. Enter the special SEIU Promo Code (provided on the SEIU Member Benefits page) when you enter your payment information.
  4. You will see a $0.01 deduction on your membership cost after the promo code is entered, indicating the code has been accepted.

Please Note: To qualify for your $40 or $20 Digital Costco Shop Card, you must agree to auto renewal of your new Costco Executive or Gold Star membership on a Visa® card at the time of sign-up.

Terms & Conditions

Valid only for new members and those whose memberships (Primary and Household) have been expired at least 18 months or more. Valid only for nonmembers for their first year of membership. Not valid for renewal or upgrade of an existing membership. Promotion may not be combined with any other promotion. Digital Costco Shop Card will be emailed to the email address provided by the Primary Member at time of sign-up within 2 weeks after successful sign-up and enrollment in auto renewal.
