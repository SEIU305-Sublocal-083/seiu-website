Oregon ERB Guide

Understanding the Employment Relations Board process under Oregon public sector labor law.

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Status: Draft for internal review

Purpose: Member-facing guide, not legal advice

Verification note: Numeric limits and contacts can change; confirm on official links before acting.

What ERB Does

The Oregon Employment Relations Board (ERB) handles public-sector labor matters under PECBA, including unfair labor practice (ULP) complaints, representation disputes, and bargaining-unit matters. ERB is a state agency, not your union representative.

Key Limits and Filing Rules

  • ULP filing deadline: complaints must be filed within 180 days of the alleged violation (ORS 243.672(6)).
  • Filing fee: ERB charges a $300 fee for a complaint and a $300 fee for an answer (OAR 115-080-0050).
  • Hearing timeline: when ERB finds an issue of fact/law, a hearing is generally set within 20 days after service of the complaint (ORS 243.676(1)(c)).

Important: If you wait too long, ERB may dismiss a valid claim as untimely. Contact your steward immediately when a possible ULP occurs.

When ERB May Apply

  • Retaliation tied to protected union activity.
  • Interference with organizing or bargaining rights.
  • Bad-faith bargaining allegations.
  • Representation/election disputes involving bargaining units.

Before Filing: Member Checklist

  1. Contact your steward first.
  2. Build a timeline with exact dates.
  3. Save emails, directives, meeting notes, and witness information.
  4. Identify the remedy you are requesting (e.g., cease-and-desist, make-whole relief).
  5. Coordinate ERB strategy and grievance strategy together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I file both a grievance and an ERB complaint? +

Sometimes yes. They are different processes with different standards and deadlines. Coordinate through your union so one track does not harm the other.

What if I am not sure the conduct is a ULP? +

Document facts and dates anyway, then contact your steward quickly. The 180-day clock keeps running while you decide.

Can ERB award back pay or other remedies? +

ERB can order statutory remedies when it finds violations, depending on case facts and legal authority.
