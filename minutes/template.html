Meeting Minutes

General Membership Meeting | August 28, 2025

Public Summary

These minutes represent a summary of the meeting for public viewing. Due to the sensitive nature of some discussions, certain details have been omitted. A more detailed, redacted version is available to members upon request.

Meeting Details

Date: August 28, 2025

Location: Memorial Union, Room 104

Meeting Called to Order: 6:02 PM by President Russ Born

Meeting Adjourned: 7:48 PM

Summary of Discussions & Decisions

Approval of Previous Minutes

Minutes from the previous meeting on [Date of Previous Meeting] were presented. A motion to approve was made, seconded, and passed without opposition.

Officer Reports

The President provided an update on the successful contract ratification and thanked the Contract Action Team. The Treasurer presented the quarterly financial report, which was reviewed and accepted by the members present.

Bargaining Update

Discussion centered on the implementation of the new contract. Key topics included the timeline for wage increases appearing on paychecks and new language regarding workplace safety protocols. Members asked clarifying questions which were addressed by the Chief Steward.

New Business

  • A motion was raised to form a new committee focused on social media and member communication. After a brief discussion about goals and volunteer needs, the motion was seconded and passed.
  • Members discussed upcoming steward elections and the importance of having representation in all departments.

Key Motions Passed

  • Motion 2025-08-01: To approve the minutes from the previous meeting. (Passed)
  • Motion 2025-08-02: To establish a volunteer Communications Social Media Team. (Passed)
