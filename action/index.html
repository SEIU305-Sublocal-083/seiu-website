Take action with our union: Tell OSU HELL NO!

Our collective power comes from members showing up. Management's latest offer includes no COLAs for four years, a 19-year path from step 1 to step 10, and proposed rollbacks to our layoff and contracting-out rights. Email OSU President Jayathi Murthy today and demand a contract that respects our work. Every email shows OSU that members and supporters are paying attention.

Send your email now, then ask one coworker to stand with us today.
