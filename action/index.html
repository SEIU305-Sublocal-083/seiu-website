Sign the Higher Ed strike pledge

The pledge tells our bargaining team and management that Higher Ed workers reject 0% COLA and the takeaways still on the table. It helps our union measure readiness and decide how strongly workers are prepared to act together.

This is not a strike-authorization vote. Sign the pledge, then ask 10 coworkers to sign with you.
