Confidential bargaining phase

Show up for the July 16 membership meeting

The bargaining team is in confidential negotiations, so the details from the table stay internal for now. What we can do publicly is build member readiness: attend the meeting, bring a coworker, join the CAT, and be ready to show OSU that higher ed workers are organized.

After the membership meeting, the CAT meets Monday evening, July 20, to coordinate coworker outreach and next steps.
