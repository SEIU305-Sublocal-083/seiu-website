Your privacy on local083.org

Privacy and analytics

We use limited website analytics to understand which public pages and resources are useful. We do not use session replay, record what you type, build advertising profiles or sell personal information.

What we collect

  • Page paths and page titlesWe remove search terms, email parameters and page fragments from analytics URLs.
  • Intentional interactionsWe count selected navigation, event, download and contact-link clicks.
  • Basic device contextOur analytics provider may process browser, device and approximate network information needed to provide aggregate reports.
  • A browser-level identifierPostHog may store a pseudonymous identifier in local storage unless analytics is disabled.

What we intentionally do not collect

  • No session recordings or replay.
  • No broad automatic click or text capture.
  • No form submissions, passwords or text entered into fields.
  • No personal profiles for advertising.
  • No sale of visitor information.

Service and external links

We use PostHog to process aggregate website analytics. Links to membership forms, action tools, Oregon State University and other services have their own privacy practices. A link click may be counted before you leave local083.org, but we do not receive the information you submit on those external services through this website.
