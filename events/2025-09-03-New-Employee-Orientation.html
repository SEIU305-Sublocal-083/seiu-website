New Employee Orientation

Learn about your rights and the benefits of becoming a member of our union.

About this Event

Welcome to Oregon State University! This orientation is for all new employees to learn about SEIU Local 083. We'll cover the benefits of membership, your rights under our contract, and how you can get involved.

Our Collective Bargaining Agreement with management allows you to attend this meeting on paid time to learn about becoming a member of our union. Please register using the link, and you will be emailed a Zoom link to join the meeting.
