Local 083 Executive Team Meeting

The monthly business meeting for our local's elected leadership.

About this Event

This is the regular monthly meeting for the SEIU Local 083 Executive Team, held on the 2nd Thursday of each month. The team discusses and makes decisions on the business of our local union.

If you are not an elected member of the Executive Team, we welcome you to attend and observe. However, please note that non-members are not allowed to vote if a vote is called during the meeting.

Please note: A link to the meeting minutes will be posted here after the meeting has concluded.
