University Day at PRAx

Volunteer with us to represent our union and connect with the OSU community.

About this Event

Join SEIU 503 at University Day, a campus-wide event at the beautiful Patricia Reser Center for the Creative Arts (PRAx). This is a fantastic opportunity for us to connect with new and current employees, share information about the benefits of our union, and build solidarity across campus.

We are looking for enthusiastic volunteers to help staff our booth. You'll answer questions, share your experience as a union member, and hand out some great SEIU swag. No experience is necessary, just a friendly face and a willingness to chat with your colleagues!

What to Expect as a Volunteer

  • Shifts are flexible. Volunteer for an hour or for the whole event.
  • We will provide all the materials and information you'll need.
  • It's a great way to meet fellow members and build community.
