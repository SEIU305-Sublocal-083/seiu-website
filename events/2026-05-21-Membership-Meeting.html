Membership Meeting

Join our union from noon to 1 p.m. in Memorial Union room 211 or on Zoom for a bargaining update.

About this Event

Please join SEIU Local 083 on Thursday, May 21, 2026, from noon to 1 p.m. in Memorial Union room 211 or on Zoom.

If you want to know what is really happening in bargaining, this is the meeting to attend. Management is putting takeaways on the table, and members need to hear what those proposals mean for our coworkers, our contract, and our ability to protect the standards we have fought for.

We will talk through where bargaining stands, what is at stake, and how we are preparing to fight back together. Bring your questions, bring a coworker, and make sure your work area is represented.
