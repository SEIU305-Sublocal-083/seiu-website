August 28, 2025

General Membership Meeting

Join us to hear updates from leadership, vote on important union business, and connect with fellow members.

About this Event

This is our quarterly General Membership Meeting, a crucial event for every member of SEIU Local 503 at OSU. It's our chance to come together as a community, discuss the issues that matter, and make democratic decisions about the future of our union.

Whether you're a new employee or a long-time member, your voice is essential. We will cover recent wins, ongoing campaigns, and preview the challenges and opportunities ahead.

Agenda

  • 5:30 PM: Doors Open & Social Time
  • 6:00 PM: Call to Order & Welcome
  • 6:15 PM: Officer Reports (President, Treasurer)
  • 6:45 PM: Bargaining Update & Discussion
  • 7:15 PM: New Business & Member Q&A
  • 7:45 PM: Adjournment
