Higher Ed Mediation Update

Hear what happened in mediation, what the latest developments mean for members and what comes next in bargaining.

About this event

Our SEIU Local 503 Higher Ed Bargaining Team will share an update on mediation and where negotiations go from here. Members who want to understand where bargaining stands and what comes next should join.

What you’ll hear

  • Hear what happened during mediation.
  • Learn what the latest developments could mean for members.
  • Find out what comes next and how members can help win a fair contract.

What is bargaining?

Bargaining is how our union negotiates with university management over pay, benefits, working conditions and the protections in our contract. During mediation, a neutral mediator helps our bargaining team and management work toward an agreement.
