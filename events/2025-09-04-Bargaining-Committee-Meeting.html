Thursday, September 4, 2025

Bargaining Committee Meeting

The regular monthly strategy meeting for the elected Bargaining Committee.

About this Event

The elected Bargaining Committee meets on the 1st Thursday of each month to prepare for and strategize about contract negotiations with OSU management.

This is a closed meeting for elected committee members to ensure confidentiality and focus. Committee members will receive a link to join via Zoom, and the team will decide if the meeting will be in-person or hybrid.

Event Information

Date & Time

Thursday, September 4, 2025

Location

Hybrid (In-person/Zoom)
Details sent to committee members.

Audience

Elected Committee Members Only
