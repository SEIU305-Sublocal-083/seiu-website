Tuesday, March 31, 2026

OSU Bargaining Rally

Join us at the MU Quad from 12:00-1:00 PM to show our support for the bargaining team and a fair contract.

Overview

Our initial OSU bargaining sessions are scheduled for March 30-31, with representatives seated at the bargaining table on campus.

Save the date and join us for a rally from 12:00-1:00 PM at the MU Quad on Tuesday, March 31. We will show our support for the bargaining team and demonstrate that we are ready to stand up for a fair contract.
