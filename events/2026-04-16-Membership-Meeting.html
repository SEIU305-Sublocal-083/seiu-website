Membership Meeting

Join our union from noon to 1 p.m. in MU 211 and RSVP through the new form so we can plan food orders and attendance.

About this Event

Our membership meeting is one of the ways we stay connected, hear the latest updates, and move our union forward together. Join SEIU Local 083 on Thursday, April 16, 2026, from noon to 1 p.m. in MU 211.

This RSVP uses a new form workflow we are testing so we can better plan food orders and get a more accurate picture of in-person and virtual attendance. After you submit the form, you will be added to the Outlook calendar invite and receive confirmation.

The Zoom link and final agenda will be added closer to the meeting. If you are planning to attend virtually, RSVP now so you receive the calendar invite when that information is ready.

Why RSVP now

  • It helps us plan food for everyone attending in person.
  • It helps us estimate virtual and in-person turnout more accurately.
  • It adds you to the Outlook calendar invite and sends confirmation after submission.
  • It makes sure you are in the loop when the Zoom link and agenda are finalized.
