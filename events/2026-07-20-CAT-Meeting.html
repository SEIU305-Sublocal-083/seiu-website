About this Event

Join SEIU Local 083 for the Monday, July 20 Contract Action Team meeting in the evening. Time and Zoom details are coming shortly.

CAT meetings are where members coordinate outreach, share updates with coworkers, and help move union action across worksites. This meeting follows the July 16 membership meeting and will focus on carrying the update back into our departments.

If you want to help but are not sure what to do next, this is the meeting to attend.
