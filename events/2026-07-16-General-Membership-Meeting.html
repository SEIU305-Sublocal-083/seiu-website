About this Event

Room change: Meet in MU 215

The Memorial Union double-booked our usual room, MU 211, and found an alternate space for us in MU 215. We have ordered TOGO'S sandwiches for lunch. Read the full room-change notice.

Join SEIU Local 083 for our General Membership Meeting on Thursday, July 16, from noon to 1 p.m. in Memorial Union room 215.

Please head directly to room 215. Zoom details are coming shortly.

We are entering a new phase of bargaining where table details are confidential for now. What remains public is our strength: higher ed works because we work. We need members ready to show OSU and higher ed that our power is disciplined, organized, and visible.

What we will cover

  • What confidential bargaining means for member updates and public messaging.
  • How members can stay ready and connected while table details stay internal.
  • How to talk with coworkers about this new phase without overpromising or guessing.
  • How the Monday, July 20 CAT meeting will help coordinate the next round of coworker outreach.
