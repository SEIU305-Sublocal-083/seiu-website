Tuesday, June 30, 2026

Rally June 30 at McNary Field

Join coworkers from noon to 1 p.m. for food, lawn games, and a strong show of unity for real raises and a fair contract.

About this Event

Management is offering no raises and no cost-of-living adjustment for four years. That is not a fair contract, and we need a strong turnout to show that members are organized, united, and ready to fight for better.

Join coworkers on Tuesday, June 30, from noon to 1 p.m. at McNary Field. We will have food, lawn games, and a clear message: OSU workers deserve real raises and a fair contract.

  • Sign up now so we can plan food, turnout, and outreach.
  • Wear purple and bring one coworker with you.
  • Arrive a few minutes early so we can gather before noon.

Event Information

Date & Time

Tuesday, June 30, 2026
12:00 PM - 1:00 PM

McNary Field

What to expect

Food, lawn games, and coworkers standing together.
