Monday, June 29, 2026

Sign-Making Party

Drop by Westminster House on Monroe from 5 to 7 p.m. to help make signs before Tuesday's McNary Field rally.

About this Event

Before we fill McNary Field on Tuesday, we need to make the rally visible.

Come make signs on Monday, June 29, from 5 to 7 p.m. at Westminster House on Monroe. Bring a coworker, bring ideas, or just show up.

This does not need to be fancy. We need signs that are direct and readable: real raises, fair contract, protect layoff rights, OSU works because we work.

  • Drop by for 20 minutes or stay the whole time.
  • Bring a phrase if you have one.
  • Invite one coworker who can help make Tuesday visible.
