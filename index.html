About SEIU 503 at OSU

SEIU Local 503 represents thousands of dedicated classified staff at Oregon State University. Our members work not only on the main campus in Corvallis but also at the Hatfield Marine Science Center in Newport, the Portland Center, and in OSU's extension offices in every county across Oregon. We are the administrative staff, food service workers, custodians, IT professionals, and many others who keep this university running. Our mission is to build power for workers to ensure fair treatment, secure good contracts, and advocate for a just and equitable workplace.

What We Do

Defend Your Rights

We ensure management respects our contract and protects members from unfair treatment through our trained steward network and legal support.

Negotiate Strong Contracts

We bargain collectively for fair wages, affordable healthcare, a secure retirement, and safe working conditions for all classified staff.

Build Worker Power

Through organizing and collective action, we build the power necessary to make OSU a more just and equitable place for everyone. Our power is in our numbers and solidarity.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are my union dues used?

Your dues fund the resources we need to win strong contracts and defend our rights. This includes legal support, skilled negotiators, organizers, training for stewards, and communications to keep all members informed.

What is the difference between a member and a non-member?

All classified staff are covered by a contract, but only members have the right to vote on that contract, elect union leadership, and participate in union decision-making. Our strength comes from a high membership rate, which shows management we are united.

How do I contact my union steward?

Your steward is your first point of contact for workplace issues. You can reach out to the stewards by emailing them from the contact page.
