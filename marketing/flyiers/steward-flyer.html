A steward is a trained coworker who represents our union on the job. Contact our steward team when you need help enforcing our contract or protecting your rights at work.

Weingarten Rights: You have the right to union representation in a meeting with management when you believe the meeting could lead to discipline. Clearly state that you want a steward present.

Questions about our contract?

Email our steward team for help understanding your rights and working conditions.

Called into a meeting?

Contact a steward before answering questions if the meeting could lead to discipline.

Facing a workplace issue?

Share a short timeline and relevant documents so a trained steward can follow up.

Need Representation?

Use our central steward email
