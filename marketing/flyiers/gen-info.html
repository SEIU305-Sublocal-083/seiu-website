We Are SEIU 503 at OSU

Our union is strongest when members lead together.

SEIU 503
Local 083

Our union is made up of classified staff who keep OSU running every day. Together we fight for respect, fair pay, and safe working conditions for everyone covered by our contract.

Our members support students, faculty, and research in Corvallis, Newport, Portland, and in extension offices across Oregon.

Solidarity

We are stronger together. When one member faces a problem, our union shows up.

Equity

We organize for fairness and dignity for all workers, across all job classes.

Democracy

Our union is member-run. Every member has a voice and a vote in our direction.

Integrity

We act transparently and stay accountable to our members in every decision.

Build Worker Power

If you are not yet a member, join us. Members vote on contracts, elect leadership, and shape what our union fights for next.
