- Campaign name: Member Control Email Preferences
- Primary goal: Get members to update `|UPDATE_PROFILE|` so our union emails better match the updates, role, and worksite that matter to them.

## Profile Fields to Explain in Every Variant
- `Communication Type`
  - `Newsletter`: general union news and roundup updates.
  - `Membership Meeting Reminders`: notices and reminders for union membership meetings.
  - `Bargaining Updates`: updates from our bargaining campaign and bargaining team.
  - `Events`: trainings, socials, rallies, and other union events.
  - `Action Alerts`: urgent asks to show up, sign, contact, or take action.
- `Member Status`
  - `Active Member`: dues-paying member status.
  - `Non-dues-paying member`: represented coworker who is not currently a dues-paying member.
  - `Steward/Leadership`: steward or leadership role for more role-relevant updates.
  - `No longer represented but still in the loop`: former represented coworkers who still want union updates.
- `Worksite - City`
  - `Corvallis Campus`
  - `Portland Campus`
  - `Bend Campus`
  - `Hatfield`
  - `Extensions Offices`
- Worksite note: worksite selection helps make updates more relevant to a member's campus or worksite.
