## Required Checks
- The broad list version leads with context and logistics.
- The engaged version leads with action and uses a higher-commitment ask.
- The disengaged version uses one message and one low-friction CTA.
- Disengaged copy does not use guilt-based framing.
- Each segment has a distinct success metric tied to its role.
