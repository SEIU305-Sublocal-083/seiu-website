A welcome from your sublocal president

Hi Carly Persons,

Welcome to our union. I am glad you are here and excited to welcome another coworker from Student Health Services into Local 083.

The work you do in Student Health Services matters. That team helps keep OSU students healthy, supported, and able to stay focused on school, and our union is stronger when coworkers from every part of campus are connected.

You are part of the classified staff union at OSU. SEIU Local 503, Local 083, represents classified staff across campus. We are member-led and built to have each other's backs at work.

As president of our SEIU Local 503 sublocal at OSU, I want you to know that you can reach out any time with questions about your rights, our contract, union membership, or how to get involved.
