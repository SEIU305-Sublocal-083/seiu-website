Hi everyone,

Management has proposed eliminating Hardship Leave. Hardship Leave is a member-to-member safety net that lets coworkers donate leave to someone facing a devastating temporary medical crisis.

If you do not agree with that proposal, this is the moment to show it. Sign up now and join us at the MU Quad next Tuesday while bargaining is happening on campus.

Why this matters

Management is saying Hardship Leave is no longer necessary. Our union strongly disagrees, and a visible crowd on March 31 is a practical way to show that members are paying attention and do not support taking this benefit away.
