Hi everyone,

Please join our union tomorrow, Thursday, May 21, 2026, from noon to 1 p.m. for our membership meeting in Memorial Union room 211 or on Zoom.

If you want to know what is really happening in bargaining, this is the meeting to attend. Management is putting takeaways on the table, and members need to hear what those proposals mean for our coworkers, our contract, and our ability to protect the standards we have fought for.
