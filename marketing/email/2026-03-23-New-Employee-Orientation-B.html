Welcome to Oregon State University,

Our union represents classified employees at OSU. New employee orientation is where we explain what that means in practice, what rights and resources are already available to you, and how to get connected early.

If you are within your first two months at OSU, this orientation time is paid. Join us on Zoom April 1 for a practical introduction to your union and the support available to you here.
