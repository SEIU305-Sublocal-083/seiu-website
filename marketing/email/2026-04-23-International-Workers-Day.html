Hi everyone,

On Friday, May 1, 2026, workers across Oregon will gather for International Workers' Day to stand in solidarity for workers' rights and demand an end to mass deportations.

SEIU 503 is primarily supporting the PCUN-hosted Salem event at the Oregon State Capitol. Portland-area actions and transportation support from Portland and Eugene are also part of the plan, and registration will help you get updates on the option that works best for you.

If you can be there with us, please register now so you receive the latest information on Salem, Portland-area actions, and other statewide updates.
