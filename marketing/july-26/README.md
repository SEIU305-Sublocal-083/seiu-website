# Local 083 July 2026 campaign scaffold

Status: scaffold only. These files are campaign-prep drafts, not public pages and not scheduled email sends.

Campaign shape:
- Website/news carries most of the education and SEO work.
- Broad email stays near one per week plus true event reminders.
- Every email should include one short Meet the Leader bio module.
- Week 1 includes a Meet the Organizer post for the new organizer.

Folders:
- `website/`: dated scaffolds for news posts, event pages, Workday posts, bargaining explainers, leader posts, and social/website copy.
- `email/`: dated scaffolds for Mailchimp email drafts.

Naming rule:
- Every scaffold starts with `YYYY-MM-DD-`.
- Website scaffolds use `website` or `event` in the filename.
- Email scaffolds use `email` in the filename.
- Each scaffold names the intended eventual publish target.

- OSU bargaining
- OSU classified workers
- SEIU Local 083
- OSU contract negotiations
