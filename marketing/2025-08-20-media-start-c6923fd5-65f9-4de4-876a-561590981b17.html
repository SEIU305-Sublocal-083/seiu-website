Amplify Our Voice!
Help us build a stronger online community by joining our new Social Media Strategy Team.

Hi everyone,

Our strength as a union comes from our ability to communicate, organize, and stand together. To better connect with members and share our stories, we are forming a volunteer Communications Social Media Team, and we need your help!

We're looking for creative and passionate members who are interested in:

  • Brainstorming engaging content ideas.
  • Helping to plan posts for Facebook and other platforms.
  • Sharing important updates and celebrating our victories online.

No professional experience is necessary! Whether you're a social media pro or just have great ideas, your voice is valuable. This is a fantastic opportunity to contribute to our union's power in a flexible and collaborative way.
