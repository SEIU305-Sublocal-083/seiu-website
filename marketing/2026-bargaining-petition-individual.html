Fighting for Higher Ed

2026 Contract Petition

SEIU Local 083

As classified workers at Oregon’s Public Universities, we are the foundation of higher education. We unite in support of our Union’s bargaining priorities:

  • Economics Fair wages that keep up with the cost of living.
  • Protections Affordable health insurance and strong PERS contributions.
  • Workload Sustainable workloads, remote work equity, and employment protections.
  • Union Power Mandatory New Employee Orientations and union communication access.

The Pledge

"We, the undersigned, are passionate about the mission of higher education and dedicated to maintaining an environment where students can thrive and succeed. Therefore, we urge our university administration to acknowledge the concerns raised by our elected bargaining team and to reach a fair contract that truly values our contributions."
