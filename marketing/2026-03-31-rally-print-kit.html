March 31 rally print kit

Organizer-ready print pieces for our 300-member turnout push at the MU Quad. Use these desk drops and wearables to reach coworkers, then send every RSVP to our union's rally signup.

How to use this kit

Use the single rally leaflet for direct coworker outreach and the wearable sheet for fast conversation starters across campus. The goal is to make the rally feel visible, urgent, and easy to join.

Each printable page opens as its own sheet so you can print only what your team needs. Keep printing set to 100% scale for the quarter-page flyers and the 2.25-inch wearable circles.
