# Final stakeholder walkthrough

Run this on the production-preview URL before launch. Do not coach participants. Ask each person to narrate what they expect, then record where they hesitate or choose the wrong path.

Record the device, browser, assistive technology (if any), completion result, time, and notes for every task.

## Classified worker

1. Find what to do if a meeting could lead to discipline.
2. Find a union steward and begin an email requesting help.
3. Find the next membership meeting and add it to a calendar.
4. Explain, in their own words, the current bargaining status after reading the bargaining page.

## Union steward

1. Find the current contract and the layoff workflow.
2. Verify that urgent guidance clearly distinguishes general information from individual advice.
3. Find the contact route for workplace representation.
4. Identify any statement that could create an incorrect deadline or procedural expectation.

## Union leader or content owner

1. Confirm every listed officer, title and shared email address.
2. Verify membership totals, county totals, bargaining figures and event logistics against source records.
3. Find the newest news story, bargaining update and past event without using site search.
4. Confirm who owns the next update and its review date using `EDITORIAL_GOVERNANCE.md`.

## First-time visitor or press contact

1. Explain what Local 083 is and whom it represents.
2. Find a public or media contact.
3. Find a factual bargaining summary and distinguish it from an action-oriented story.
4. Locate an accessible description of a recent union event.

## Acceptance criteria

- Each critical task is completed without guidance.
- No participant encounters a dead end, ambiguous contact route or unexplained union term.
- A worker can reach urgent rights guidance in no more than two choices from the homepage or Resources page.
- A first-time visitor can identify the represented group and current bargaining status accurately.
- Any severe issue is fixed and retested with the same task before launch.
