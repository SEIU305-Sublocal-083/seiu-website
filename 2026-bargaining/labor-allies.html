For Labor Allies

Bring visible labor solidarity to McNary Field.

Unions, worker organizations, labor councils, and workplace activists can help classified staff make June 30 a stronger public action. The ask is practical: show up, bring people, share the event, and keep the focus on a fair contract for OSU classified staff.

Sign Up for the Rally Use the Toolkit

What allies can do

  • Bring members or coworkers to the rally.
  • Share the event link in your networks.
  • Bring signs or banners that support fair wages, affordable healthcare, job security, and worker respect.
  • Use clear language that supports classified staff without speaking over them.

Keep the message aligned

The main message is that OSU classified staff deserve real raises, affordable healthcare, strong job protections, and a fair contract. Allies do not need to add complexity. A visible, disciplined show of support helps management see that the offer is unacceptable to more than one group of workers.

Useful phrase: "Labor stands with OSU classified staff for real raises, affordable healthcare, and a fair contract."

Before you post

If your organization has not formally endorsed the event, use individual support language or ask your group before posting as an official account. This keeps support accurate and avoids creating claims the local has not made.
