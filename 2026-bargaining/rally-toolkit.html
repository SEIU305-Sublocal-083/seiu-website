Help build turnout for the OSU bargaining rally.

Use these links, sample messages, and quick outreach ideas to bring coworkers, students, faculty, graduate workers, families, community members, and labor allies to McNary Field on Tuesday, June 30.

Sample social post

OSU classified staff keep the university running. Management is offering no step increases, no COLAs, higher healthcare costs, and weaker protections. Show up Tuesday, June 30, noon to 1 p.m. at McNary Field to support real raises and a fair contract. Sign up: local083.org/rally

Sample email or text

Can you come with me to the SEIU Local 083 rally on Tuesday, June 30? It is noon to 1 p.m. at McNary Field. Classified staff are bargaining against a no-raise offer and higher healthcare costs. A strong turnout matters. Signup is at local083.org/rally.

What to bring

  • Wear purple if you can.
  • Bring a coworker, classmate, friend, or family member.
  • Bring water and weather-appropriate clothing.
  • Use a sign with a clear message: real raises, fair contract, affordable healthcare, respect classified staff.
